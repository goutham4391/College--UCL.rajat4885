For businesses

        

      Whether you’re a global company, SME, third sector organisation or local authority, we have the people and processes that can get you connected to world-changing ideas and innovations.    

        

          

    

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            
        

                      

                                            

                  Develop a partnership with UCL
                

                                            
                  
                
                            

                                

                
Open up a world of possibilities for your organisation through a strategically managed, long-term research partnership.
              

            

                  

                              
        

                      

                                            

                  SMEs: Innovate and expand into new markets
                

                                            
                  
                
                            

                                

                
Get the advice and connections you need to scale up and grow internationally.
              

            

                  

                                    

                      
        

                      

                                            

                  Grow your business through a Knowledge Transfer Partnership (KTP)
                

                                            
                  
                
                            

                                

                
Take your business to the next level with the academic know-how and funding that brings about real change.
              

            

                  

                              
        

                      

                                            

                  Find a consultant
                

                                            
                  
                
                            

                                

                
Connect with the academic experts who can solve problems, realise opportunities and develop the skills in your business to manage complex issues. 
              

            

                  

                                    

                      
        

                      

                                            

                  Access UCL's technologies
                

                                            
                  
                
                            

                                

                
Connect your business to innovations that have the power to change the world with the opportunity to license our technologies.
              

            

                  

                              
        

                      

                                            

                  Bespoke short courses
                

                                            
                  
                
                            

                                

                
Equip your teams with the new perspectives and skills needed to keep your organisation moving ahead, through custom-made training programmes.
              

            

                  

                                    

                      
        

                      

                                            

                  Access our graduates and students
                

                                            
                  
                
                            

                                

                
Attract and recruit the best talent from UCL, with opportunities to meet students, promote your organisation, arrange internships and advertise vacancies.
